Famous Curly Hair Influencers

Having natural can be an extraordinary yet a confusing journey. It is never clear if it is okay or not to have natural hair and even embracing it. That is why it is always important to have some kind of inspiration to educate today's society that hair does not define a person. Here is a list of some of the most famous and inspiring influencers in natural hair on social media.

These ladies are known for their hard work and patience on growing their hair after damage and stereotype.

Bianca Reene

Bianca started as a Youtuber back in 2010, she talked about hair and hair care and soon after that a year later she became known for her amazing and healthy looking curls. Bianca has inspired many girls and even guys around the world to love and embrace the beauty of having natural curly hair. On one of her videos she stated "You are beautiful when you have straight hair or when you have curly hair. If you like your straight hair more than your curly hair that's fine, as long as you still love and know that you are beautiful with your curly hair as well." which portraits her support for people's decisions on wether having curly or straight hair is okay, as long as you still love yourself naturally.

Vanessa VanDyke

Vanessa VanDyke was a student at Faith Christian Academy at Orlando, Florida back in 2015. The teenage girl suffered mocking and bullying from students at her old school regarding to the way she wore her hair and the way it looked. As a result, the girl blew up on the news and social media. The school had a strict dress code in which included the way that the student should wear their hair, in which it specifically stated "no student is allowed to have dyed hair or anything that can cause a distraction" in which caused a controversy, where people had opinions agaisnt or for the decision wether she was going to be allowed to continue to study at the Academy. This issue is still going on, the debate of wether it is appropriate for girls or even guys to wear their natural hair on a workplace or is considered unprofessional to wear in professional environments. She pursued wearing her hair naturally and embraced it.

Sayria Jade

Sayria is a very well known youtuber. She started her youtube career back in 2012. Her youtube content consists of hair care, skin care and beauty. she is also well known for her great journey on natural hair, back in 2012 her hair wasn't the best and as she continued to persevire, she got to her major goal which was to have her hair healthy. She is now considered as a role model for other girls who have problem being comfortable in their own skin.
